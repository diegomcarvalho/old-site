Tips for Data Science and Production Engineer Students

Diego Carvalho
2 July 2021

Introduction

During the last years, my students commonly ask me about several technics turning into the mainstream in our daily engineering work. Among these technics, buzzy words like data mining, artificial intelligence, data analysis, statistics, data visualization, big data, etc., are overused in the media and advertisements.

Typically, there are some knowledge gaps my students need to fill before actively engaging in the use of these technics. So, I decided to prepare a set of tips to enter this world where data empowers our decision ability.

So, if you are ready to join this fantastic world, I divided my tips into groups where each level represents one higher stage in the student's formation path. Please, don't forget that each previous level is fundamental to achieving the next.
